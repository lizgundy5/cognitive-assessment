# 🧠 Cognitive Function Assessment

A multi-method cognitive function assessment that goes deeper than the standard 4-letter MBTI. Built on Jungian cognitive functions — not just behavior, but *how your mind actually works*.

**Live:** [lizgundy5.github.io/cognitive-assessment](https://lizgundy5.github.io/cognitive-assessment)

---

## What Makes It Different

Most personality tests ask "do you like parties?" and call it a day. This one uses **six independent methods** to catch what self-reporting alone misses:

| Method | What It Measures |
|---|---|
| **🧩 Forced Choice (32)** | Head-to-head function pairings with a diagnostic weight matrix — every question knows which types it discriminates |
| **📊 Behavioral Frequency** | What you actually *do*, not what you think you are |
| **⚡ Stress Patterns** | Functions regress predictably under pressure — reveals your inferior function |
| **🔋 Energy & Processing** | Separates social *skill* from social *energy* — critical for catching introverts who test as extraverts |
| **🪞 Ideal-Self Gap** | The gap between who you are and who you wish you were — large gaps reveal suppressed functions |
| **✍️ Free Response NLP** | Language patterns the numbers miss — word choice, frustration direction, best-self context |

## The Energy Problem

Two people can score identically on Fe (Extraverted Feeling) questions. One is an Fe-dom who *recharges* through people. The other uses Fe skillfully but *needs solitude*. This assessment catches that — the energy mismatch detection is often the difference between an accurate type and a mistype.
